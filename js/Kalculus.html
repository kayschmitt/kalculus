About Me

Hi, my name is Kayla Schmitt and I am a senior Computer Science major at the University of Missouri. Along with being a student I've also been tutoring calculus for the University for 3 years. In my experience I have found that there are so many students that take calculus, but not nearly enough resources online to fit the demand. I have built this site to help students with several calculus concepts. This site follows the book Single Variable Calculus, 7th edition, which can be purchaced here
I'm going to easily explain some topics that I have found my students to have the most difficulty with. This site is not supposed to be used as a supstitute to a textbook or course but rather a suppliment to go over things that you have already learned. I hope you enjoy!
